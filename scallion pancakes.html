scallion pancakes / 葱油饼


what is it?

scallion pancakes are salty, doughy slices of goodness. a staple breakfast food in china, scallion pancakes can often be found in food stalls and enjoyed by everyone!


ingredients

  • all-purpose flour
  • boiling water
  • salt
  • scallion stalks
  • sesame oil
  • vegetable oil

directions

  1. place the flour in a bowl and mix it with water slowly. roll the mixture into a ball and knead it for a good 4-6 minutes before leaving it to rest for half an hour in the bowl. make sure to cover the bowl with plastic wrap when letting it rest.
  2. divide the dough into fist-sized sections and flatten out into a circle. brush the top of the dough with sesame oil and sprinkle with salt and chopped scallions. roll up the dough into a rope shape and coil it, pinching the end of the rope when reaching the end of the dough.
  3. in a skillet, pour in some vegetable oil and place the pancakes in, letting them cook until golden brown on both sides.

and you're done!
